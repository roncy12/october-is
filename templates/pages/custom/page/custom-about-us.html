Who We Are

Hello, we’re Icon Sleep – one of the industry’s pioneers in selling mattresses-in-a-box. You may not have heard of us (unlike our more popular contemporaries), and that's for a good reason: To keep our costs low, we avoid expensive marketing gimmicks. We sell some of the best-designed mattresses, for exactly the price they should cost. Simple, isn't it?

Our Vision

After spending years of studying the science behind mattresses, our goal has become clearer: we want to see an invigorated worldfree of restlessness or sleep deprivation.

Why We’re Doing This

Our end goal has always been the same since we started: To give people great sleep and good value for every purchase they make with us. We believe we can achieve this by crafting an expertly designed mattress without sacrificing quality for profit. We also work hard to get our products tested and certified – all for an iconic sleep experience
