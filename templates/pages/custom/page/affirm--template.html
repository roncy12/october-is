What is Affirm?

Affirm is a financing alternative to credit cards and other credit payment solutions. It offers real-time financing options for purchases made online. When you pay with Affirm, you receive your purchase now and pay for it in fixed monthly installments, as if you used a credit card.

Read More

How does Affirm approve borrowers for loans?

Affirm will ask for a few, but important, pieces of information – your name, email, mobile number, date of birth, and the last four digits of your social security number to process your loan. The company uses this information to verify your identity and to be able to make a quick, real-time decision.

Read More

As a borrower, will I be credit checked?

Yes, when you first create your account with Affirm, you’ll be ‘soft’ credit checked to help verify your identity and determine your eligibility for financing. This ‘soft’ credit check will not affect your credit score.
