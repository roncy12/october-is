Do Icon Sleep bed sheets only fit Icon Sleep mattresses?

No. You don’t need an Icon Sleep mattress to use our comfy sheets. Any mattress up to 14” thick will work.

How do I choose the best pillow for me?

When shopping for pillows, there are things you need to consider such as firmness of pillow material, firmness of your mattress, your usual sleeping position, your weight and shoulder width, and loftiness of the pillow.

What size of bed sheets do I need?

Icon Sleep mattress sheets are labeled to match the corresponding mattress size.
