Frequently Asked Questions

What does a mattress topper do?

A mattress topper sits on top of your mattress and provides additional support that prolongs a mattress’ lifespan by reducing pressure on the base foams.

What should I look for in a mattress topper?

A good mattress topper must be sufficiently thick to prevent or delay sagging of the mattress beneath. If you wish to have cooler nights, you should look for gel-infused toppers as they regulate heat and promotes breathability while you sleep.

Do you need to put a bedding sheet over a mattress topper?

Ideally, yes. All mattress topper users need to put a fitted sheet over them and not the other way around. If you place the topper on top of the bedding and sleeps on it directly, it could potentially reduce the topper’s lifespan.
