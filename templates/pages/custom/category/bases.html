Frequently Asked Questions

Do Icon Sleep mattresses work on adjustable bases?

Yes, all Icon Sleep mattresses are compatible with adjustable beds.

Is bed base the same as a box spring?

Not really, no. While they both provide a base support layer for any mattress type, box springs add that extra “give” feeling and increase breathability. Bases, on the other hand, are for overall mattress support.

Do you really need a base for a mattress?

For mattresses to reach their intended lifespan of more or less 10 years, they need critical support that mattress bases provide. This is to keep sagging and degradation at bay.
