Hand-Finished By Italian Artisans

Hand-finished in a multi-generational confection house in Florence. these sheets demonstrate the perfection and artistry of Italian craftsmen. Italians artisans foster pride in workmanship and subtle elegance in their product: they sew, cut, and fit the sheets better than anyone else in the world

Premium Egyptian Cotton

Renowned for its soft drape, distinctive luster, and amazing durability, extra-long-staple Egyptian cotton tops the list of premium bedding materials-for good reason. Purchasing a set of luxury Egyptian cotton bedding is an investment in comfort that will last for generations.
