Open Pore Composition

Rayon from bamboo is very breathable and moisture-wicking thanks to its open pore composition. This also contributes to increased temperature regulation, which helps you stay cool and dry throughout the night.

Smooth, Round Fibers

Once the soft and smooth rayon is extracted from the bamboo fiber, it is woven in a durable twill weave that creates a beautiful drape. The smooth, round fibers give the sheets a very soft feel, making them an excellent option if you have with sensitive skin.

Fast-Growing Bamboo

Bamboo is one of the fastest growing plants in the world at a rate of up to three feet a day. When planted in its natural habitat, bamboo doesn’t require irrigation. This helps to conserve water and provide a renewable resource for premium bedding.
