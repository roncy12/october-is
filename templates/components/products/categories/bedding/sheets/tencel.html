THE BENEFITS OF TENCEL™ LYOCELL FIBERS

The 100 percent Tencel™ Lyocell fibers in this sheet set feature exceptional thermal regulation, enhanced breathability, minimal static charge, and create a beautiful drape with rich color retention. Plus, the fibers are unfavorable for bacterial growth, giving you a cleaner sleep environment.

BOTANIC ORIGIN

Tencel™ Lyocell fibers are derived from sustainable wood sources, harvested from certified and controlled sources following the stringent guidelines of the Lenzing Wood and Pulp Policy. Wood and pulp used by the Lenzing Group comes from natural forests and sustainably managed plantations. All Tencel™ Lyocell fiber types have been certified as biodegradable and compostable.

SUSTAINABLE PRODUCTION

TENCEL™ Lyocell is created in an environmentally responsible, closed-loop production process, which transforms wood pulp into cellulosic fibers with high resource efficiency and low ecological impact. This award-winning, solvent-spinning process recycles process water and reuses the solvent at a recovery rate of more than 99 percent.

MADE TO FIT BETTER

With generous, oversized dimensions and Universal Fit™ technology, our sheets were designed to securely fit all mattress styles—and look luxurious.
