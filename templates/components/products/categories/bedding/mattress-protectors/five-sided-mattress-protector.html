What is a Five 5ided® Mattress Protector?

Our Five 5ided® protectors offer superior mattress protection against the elements, are liquid-proof yet super breathable, and prevent allergens and dust mites from accumulating on all sides of your mattress.

Are your protectors waterproof and breathable?

Yes. Malouf’s protectors are equipped with a polyurethane backing that prevents unwanted leaks and spills from damaging the materials inside. The fabric used is porous and breathable.

I am a fur parent. Does owning a pet affect my warranty?

TClawed pets can puncture the waterproofing material of your mattress protector and void your warranty. We recommend keeping your clawed fur babies off the bed to extend the life your protector.

Can I use a heating pad with this mattress protector?

It depends on the heat settings. Extreme heat can compromise the integrity of the polyurethane backing that waterproofs the product. You may use a heating pad no longer than 30 minutes at a time and set the heat to low-to-medium.
