Can I use my existing mattress with an adjustable bed base?

Before purchasing, check with the manufacturer of your mattress. Most foam- and innerspring-based mattresses work with an adjustable base. We recommend mattresses between 10” to 12” for best fitting with our bed bases.

What is Reach Assist™ on the S755 Adjustable Bed Base?

Malouf’s ultra-luxurious S750 (currently not available here at Icon Sleep) and S755 bed bases have a feature called Reach Assist™, which pulls the bed back toward the wall as you incline for easy access to your nightstand.

How does the Snore Detection feature work on the Malouf Base App™?

When a snore is detected for a definite amount of time (which can be preset by the user), the intuitive Malouf Base App™ will automatically move your adjustable bed base to the Anti-Snore position.
