FITS ANY BED SIZE

The Universal Bed Frame is highly adjustable and provides enhanced frame support for Twin, Twin XL, Full, Full XL, Queen, King, and California King bed sizes.

Product Features

All-steel construction offers exceptional support for your mattress

Longer rails and single-rail center support offer increased stability; up to 9 support legs reinforced with patented Tri-Support™ system

Choose locking rug rollers, ideal for carpeted floors; or heavy-duty glides designed for hardwood floors

Conveniently adjusts to fit Twin, Twin XL, Full, Full XL, Queen, King, and California King box springs

Built-in headboard brackets connect with most headboards

Fold Me

Moving your bed doesn't have to be a hassle. The sleek, metal frame folds in half for easy shipping and storage

Easy Setup

A truly versatile design. Place directly on foundations, existing bedframes, or the ground-whatever works best for your room. Plus, it only takes 15 minutes to set up from start to finish.
