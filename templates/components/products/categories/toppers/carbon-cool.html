What is OmniPhase®?

The OmniPhase® change material is an advanced fabric used in our specialized toppers that absorbs, stores, and releases heat as needed to maintain a comfortable sleep temperature in any environment. It is lab-rated to have 20 times more cooling capacity than gel memory foam.
