Icon Sleep designs, manufactures, and stores its mattresses – and its components – in the United States. This is to ensure that all Icon Sleep products are designed and manufactured under strict quality assurance standards as regulated and certified by CertiPUR-US® and OEKO-TEX® Standard 100.

How can Icon Sleep sell for less?

The Icon Sleep business model is brilliantly straightforward: we design a mattress, we build it, we store it, and then we sell it – exclusively – online.
