Here are some frequently asked questions.

Can you flip a hybrid mattress?

No, the typical hybrid mattress has a top-down design, meaning you’re supposed to lay on one side of the bed, while the other is made to rest on a bed base. Flipping a top-down mattress like the Weekender Hybrid can wear the bed down drastically, and is just plain uncomfortable to lay on.

Read More

Do hybrid mattresses need a box spring?

No, the Weekender Hybrid doesn’t require a box spring. In fact, box springs may harm hybrid mattresses because they are not a suitable foundation for top-down beds. REMEMBER: pairing most hybrid mattresses with a box spring may void their warranties.

Read More

Do hybrid mattresses sag?

The Weekender Hybrid is made with heavy-duty tempered coils that create a strong core and a second line of defense against sagging.
